History

TechCon's remarkable journey began in 1987 when visionary technologist Martha Chen opened her Silicon Valley garage to a small group of computing enthusiasts. What started as informal weekend discussions about emerging PC technologies has evolved into one of the world's most influential technology conferences, shaping industry trends and fostering innovation for nearly four decades. From those humble beginnings with just 12 attendees, TechCon has grown to attract over 30,000 participants annually across multiple global locations. The conference has witnessed and often predicted major technological shifts, from the early days of personal computing through the internet revolution, mobile computing, social media integration, and today's AI-driven landscape. Throughout its evolution, TechCon has maintained its founding principle of bringing together diverse voices in technology - from seasoned industry veterans to bright-eyed students, from established corporations to disruptive startups. This unique blend of perspectives continues to drive the meaningful conversations and breakthrough collaborations that define the TechCon experience.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to accelerate technological progress by connecting innovators, fostering collaboration, and showcasing breakthrough technologies that shape our future. We believe that the greatest innovations emerge when diverse minds come together to share knowledge and challenge conventional thinking.
